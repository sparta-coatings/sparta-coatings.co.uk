What Is Metal Roof Coating?
Metal roof coating is the professional spray application of high-performance, corrosion-resistant coating systems to existing metal, steel, aluminium, and profiled sheet roofs — completely restoring their waterproofing, protection, and appearance without the enormous cost and disruption of full roof replacement.

Using airless spray equipment and specialist metal roof coating systems, our trained applicators deliver a seamless, weather-tight, UV-stable finish across the entire roof surface — sealing seams, treating cut-edge corrosion, and protecting the substrate for decades.

It's suitable for all commercial and industrial metal roof types, including:

Corrugated steel and profiled metal sheet roofs

Box profile steel roofing (Trisomet, Kingspan, Corus)

Standing seam metal roofs

Plastisol-coated steel cladding roofs

Galvanised steel roofs

Aluminium sheet roofs

Composite insulated panel roofs

Fibre cement and asbestos encapsulation (specialist treatment)

The Paints & Products We Use
Metal roof coating demands an entirely different product specification from any other surface. The coating system must provide genuine corrosion inhibition, elastomeric flexibility, waterproofing, UV resistance, and adhesion to a wide range of aged, weathered, and potentially contaminated metal substrates.

Surface Treatment & Preparation Products
Industrial Degreaser / Fungicidal Wash — All roof surfaces are treated with a high-strength industrial degreaser and fungicidal wash to remove all biological growth, dirt, oil, and loose contamination before any mechanical preparation begins

3,000 PSI Industrial Pressure Washer — Used to pressure wash the entire roof surface, removing failed coatings, loose rust, biological deposits, and contamination down to a sound substrate

Rust Converter / Rust Treatment (Rust-Oleum Noxyde base preparation) — All areas of active rust and corrosion are treated with a rust converter, chemically neutralising iron oxide and creating a stable base layer before priming

Seam & Joint Sealants
Polyurethane or Butyl Mastic Sealant — Applied to all roof seams, laps, and joints before priming and topcoating. This is a critical step in eliminating water ingress pathways

Reinforcing Scrim / Polyester Fleece Tape — Embedded into the sealant at all seam lines, overlaps, bolt heads, and penetrations to provide a reinforced, flexible waterproof membrane at the most vulnerable points of the roof

Primers
Sikalastic Metal Primer (2K Anticorrosive Primer) — A two-component, anticorrosive epoxy primer specifically formulated for exposed metal and steel substrates prior to liquid waterproofing system application. Fast-curing (overcoat after 6 hours), providing exceptional corrosion protection in industrial and marine environments with outstanding adhesion to steel, aluminium, and galvanised metal

Rust-Oleum Noxyde Pegarust — A solvent-based, flexible anti-corrosion coating for iron, galvanised steel, aluminium, and zinc that functions as both primer and direct-to-metal topcoat. UV and impact resistant — one of the most trusted single-coat, direct-to-rust systems for aged metal roofs on the UK market

Zinga Cold Galvanising Compound — Applied to areas of bare steel and exposed cut edges to provide sacrificial cathodic protection — halting corrosion at the microscopic level before the main coating system is applied

Topcoats
Rust-Oleum Mathys Noxyde (Elastomeric Anti-Corrosion Topcoat) — The UK's leading professional elastomeric metal roof coating. A solvent-based, highly flexible, anti-corrosion system for steel, galvanised, aluminium, and profiled metal roofing. Applied at high build (200+ microns DFT), it creates a seamless, crack-bridging, waterproof membrane that flexes with the roof under thermal movement. Available in a range of colours including Anthracite Grey, Terracotta, Tile Red, and custom shades. Outstanding UV resistance and corrosion protection

Rust-Oleum Mathys Duramax (2K Epoxy Topcoat) — For metal roofs requiring the highest level of protection and chemical resistance, our two-component epoxy system provides a fully cured, extremely hardwearing, impermeable film with excellent adhesion to all metal substrates

Permagard Metal Roof Coating — A premium, elastomeric single-component system specifically formulated for profiled metal and corrugated steel roofs. Available with a 20-year transferable warranty, making it one of the highest-guaranteed metal roof coating systems available in the UK

Cut-Edge Corrosion Treatment System
Cut-edge corrosion is the single most common failure mode on profiled metal roofs in the UK — caused by moisture penetrating the exposed cut edges of steel sheets and attacking the core from the inside outward. Our dedicated cut-edge treatment process:

Clean and degrease all cut edges

Apply rust converter to active corrosion

Apply two coats of Zinga cold galvanising or Sikalastic primer to cut edges

Seal with polyurethane mastic and reinforcing tape

Overcoat with full elastomeric topcoat system

Our Metal Roof Coating Process
1. Free Roof Survey & Condition Report
We carry out a full roof inspection — assessing substrate condition, extent of corrosion, cut-edge failure, seam integrity, waterproofing status, and existing coating adhesion. We provide a written condition report and recommended coating specification before any work begins.

2. HSE-Compliant Safe Access
All roof work is carried out in full compliance with HSE Working at Height Regulations, with appropriate edge protection, netting, or scaffold systems in place before work commences.

3. Biocidal Wash & Industrial Cleaning
A fungicidal and biocidal wash is applied across the entire roof surface and left to dwell. The roof is then pressure washed at up to 3,000 PSI to remove all biological growth, failed coatings, loose rust, and contamination.

4. Rust & Corrosion Treatment
All rusted areas and cut edges are treated with rust converter and cold galvanising compound. Delaminating or failed existing coatings are removed down to a sound substrate.

5. Seam Sealing & Scrim Reinforcement
All seams, laps, bolt heads, penetrations, and roof lights are sealed with polyurethane mastic and reinforced with polyester scrim tape — creating a fully waterproof detail at every vulnerable point.

6. Primer Application
The appropriate primer system (Sikalastic 2K metal primer or Rust-Oleum Noxyde) is applied by airless spray across all roof surfaces, achieving a fully continuous, corrosion-inhibiting base layer.

7. Elastomeric Topcoat — Pass 1
The first full coat of the specified elastomeric roof coating is applied by professional airless spray, achieving full, even coverage across all roof profiles, including ribs, valleys, and details.

8. Elastomeric Topcoat — Pass 2
A second full coat is applied once the first has cured to the recoat window, building to the specified dry film thickness (typically 200–400 microns DFT) for a fully seamless, waterproof membrane.

9. Inspection, Audit & Warranty Sign-Off
A full project audit and quality inspection is completed on finishing. Where manufacturer warranties are issued, the system is signed off by the approved coating supplier.
