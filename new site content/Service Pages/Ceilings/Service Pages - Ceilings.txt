What Is Ceiling Spraying?
Ceiling spraying is the professional application of specialist ceiling and interior coatings by airless or HVLP spray equipment — achieving a perfectly smooth, even finish across flat, textured, or suspended ceilings without the streaks, lap marks, and uneven coverage that rollers inevitably leave.

It's the professional method of choice for large-area ceiling projects — covering significant square meterage in a fraction of the time of traditional methods, while achieving a depth and consistency of finish that is impossible to replicate with a brush or roller.

Suitable for all ceiling types, including:

Domestic plaster ceilings (smooth and textured)

Commercial office and retail ceilings

Suspended ceiling tiles and grids

Exposed concrete soffits

Industrial warehouse and factory ceilings

Ceiling voids and exposed services

Structural steel and purlins within ceiling voids

Artex and textured ceiling coatings

Fire-rated ceiling systems

The Paints & Products We Use
Ceiling coatings must provide excellent opacity, coverage, and a consistent flat or matte appearance — with specific products selected for domestic, commercial, or industrial environments.

Surface Preparation
Zinsser BullsEye 1-2-3 (Stain-Blocking Primer) — For domestic and commercial ceilings with nicotine stains, watermarks, or general discolouration — applied before the topcoat to seal and block stains for a consistent, clean final finish

Zinsser Gardz Problem Surface Sealer — For Artex, textured ceilings, and previously painted surfaces showing signs of chalking or poor adhesion — penetrates and consolidates the substrate, locking down loose particles before topcoat application

Polythene Sheeting & Heavy-Duty Masking Film — All walls, floors, furniture, and fittings are fully protected using heavy-duty masking film before any spray work commences

Topcoats

Ultra-durable, highly washable, and extremely opaque — providing a pristine flat matt finish in a single coat on correctly primed surfaces. Available in Brilliant White and tinted to any Dulux colour reference

Johnstone's Trade Covaplus Vinyl Matt — A professional, high-opacity vinyl matt ceiling coating for domestic and commercial interiors. Excellent coverage, smooth flow, and easy application by airless spray — ideal for large-area ceiling projects

Tikkurila Luja 7 / Luja 3 (Matt & Eggshell) — Premium Finnish interior coatings offering exceptional durability, washability, and a flawless finish on domestic and high-specification commercial ceilings. Very low VOC, suitable for sensitive environments including nurseries, hospitals, and schools

Our Ceiling Spraying Process
1. Survey & Preparation Assessment
We assess the ceiling type, area, height, condition, and any staining or adhesion issues. We confirm the most appropriate coating system and agree the working schedule — including out-of-hours if required.

2. Masking & Full Protection
All walls, floors, furniture, fittings, and light fittings are protected with heavy-duty polythene sheeting and masking film. For ceiling void work, all services, ducting, and equipment are fully covered before any spray work begins.

3. Surface Preparation & Priming
Stains, watermarks, and problem areas are spot-treated with Zinsser BullsEye 1-2-3 or Gardz sealer. Textured or Artex ceilings are treated with Zinsser Gardz to consolidate the surface before topcoat.

4. Mist Coat Application
A fine diluted mist coat is applied first, allowing the ceiling to accept the full-strength topcoat without absorption problems or flashing.

5. Full Coat Application
A minimum of two full coats of the specified topcoat are applied by airless or HVLP spray — achieving a perfectly smooth, even, streak-free finish across the entire ceiling area. Each coat is checked before the next is applied.

6. De-mask, Inspect & Handover
All masking is carefully removed. Walls, coving, and edges are checked for overspray. A full quality inspection is completed with you before we leave.
