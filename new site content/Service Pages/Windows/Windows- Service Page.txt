What Is UPVC Window Spraying?
UPVC window spraying is a professional on-site coating process that applies specialist, chemically-bonding Kolorbond paints directly to your existing UPVC or PVCu window frames — transforming their colour and appearance without the cost of replacement.

Unlike conventional paints, Kolorbond coatings are specifically formulated to chemically bond to UPVC and PVCu — a non-porous plastic surface that standard paints cannot adhere to. The result is a long-lasting, UV-resistant, weather-proof finish backed by a 10-year manufacturer adhesion guarantee.

Suitable for all UPVC window types, including:

Casement windows

Sash windows

Bay and bow windows

Tilt-and-turn windows

Fixed-light windows

Bi-fold and sliding windows

The Paints & Products We Use
Every Kolorbond product in our window spraying system is purpose-formulated for UPVC and PVCu — no generic primers, no exterior masonry paint, no automotive products. The system is as follows:

Stage 1 — Surface Preparation
Kolorbond WindowPrep — The manufacturer-specified, alcohol-based UPVC cleaning and preparation solution. Applied on a clean cloth across all window frame surfaces to remove silicone residue, grease, oxidisation, and atmospheric contamination. This is the non-negotiable first step in the Kolorbond application process — the manufacturer states clearly that WindowPrep must be used before any Kolorbond coating is applied to ensure adhesion

Mirka Mirlon Red Scotch-Brite Pads (or 400-grit wet-and-dry) — After WindowPrep, all UPVC surfaces are lightly abraded to create a mechanical key on the surface. This step is critical — it physically scratches the surface to give the Kolorbond coating additional mechanical grip alongside its chemical bond

Stage 2 — No Separate Primer Required
One of the key technical advantages of the Kolorbond system is that no separate primer coat is required on correctly prepared UPVC. Kolorbond Original and Aquatek bond directly to the prepared UPVC surface — eliminating an entire preparation stage and reducing project time and cost. This is only possible because of Kolorbond's proprietary chemical bonding technology, and only when the WindowPrep preparation stage has been correctly followed.

Stage 3 — Kolorbond Topcoat (By Project Type)
Kolorbond Original — The UK's market-leading, single-pack UPVC coating. Chemically bonds directly to UPVC, PVCu, and powder-coated aluminium window frames without a primer. Available in 5,000+ colours across gloss, satin, and matte finishes. UV-stable colour pigments prevent fading and chalking. Backed by a 10-year manufacturer adhesion guarantee. The professional standard for UPVC window spraying across the UK

Kolorbond Aquatek — A single-pack, water-based satin UPVC coating for residential projects where low odour and fast re-entry times are a priority. Bonds directly to UPVC without a primer (following WindowPrep preparation). Scratch and weather resistant, with excellent UV resistance. The recommended Kolorbond product for occupied residential properties

Kolorbond DecoProt (2K Polyurethane) — A two-pack, catalysed polyurethane coating for UPVC and aluminium window frames requiring maximum durability. Available in 10%, 30%, 50%, and 70% gloss levels. Chemically cures to an extremely hard, scratch-resistant, UV-stable film — specified for commercial properties and windows subject to heavy use or cleaning

Kolorbond K2 (2K Polyurethane) — The highest-performance coating in the Kolorbond range. Two-pack catalysed system providing maximum hardness, chemical resistance, UV stability, and abrasion resistance. Specified for commercial window frames, aluminium sections, and GRP surfaces requiring the best possible durability

Our UPVC Window Spraying Process
1. Free Site Survey & Colour Consultation
We assess your window frame condition, material, and advise on the correct Kolorbond product for your project. We discuss your chosen colour and confirm a silicone colour to match.

2. Silicone Removal
All existing silicone beads around the window frames are carefully removed before preparation begins — critical for achieving a clean, precise edge line.

3. Kolorbond WindowPrep Application
All UPVC frame surfaces are wiped thoroughly with Kolorbond WindowPrep, removing all grease, silicone residue, oxidisation, and contamination. Applied twice to ensure a fully clean, contamination-free substrate.

4. Mechanical Abrading (Mirka Mirlon Pads)
All prepared surfaces are lightly abraded with Mirka Mirlon red scotch-brite pads to create a consistent mechanical key across all frame profiles.

5. Masking & Protection
All glazing, brickwork, sills, and surrounding surfaces are fully protected with masking paper and professional tape — ensuring a perfectly straight spray line.

6. Mist Coat
A fine, diluted mist coat of Kolorbond is applied first — providing an initial adhesion layer and revealing any remaining surface contamination before the full coats are applied.

7. Full Coat Application (Minimum 3 Coats)
A minimum of three full coats of the specified Kolorbond product are applied using professional HVLP spray equipment, building to a consistent, even colour and finish across all frame profiles, sashes, and beads.

8. Silicone Resealing & Final Inspection
Fresh silicone is applied in your chosen RAL-matched colour. All masking is removed and a full quality inspection completed before we leave.
