What Happens Next?
Once you submit your enquiry or get in touch directly, here's what to expect:

1. We respond within 24 hours
A member of the Sparta Coatings team will contact you — usually the same day — to discuss your project and arrange a convenient time for your free survey.

2. Free site survey
We visit your property at a time that suits you. We assess the surfaces, discuss your colour and finish choices, and take all the measurements needed to provide an accurate quotation.

3. Written specification & fixed-price quote
You receive a written specification detailing exactly what products will be used, how the work will be carried out, and a fixed price with no hidden extras. No commitment required.

4. We get to work
Once you're happy to proceed, we schedule your project, agree a start date, and deliver the transformation.

Frequently Asked Questions
Is the survey really free?
Yes — completely free, with no call-out charge and no obligation to proceed. We visit, assess, and quote. The rest is entirely up to you.

How quickly can you start?
Lead times vary depending on the time of year and current workload. Contact us to discuss your preferred timescale and we'll do our best to accommodate it.

Do you work outside of your core coverage area?
For residential projects we cover Hampshire, West Sussex, East Sussex, Surrey, Dorset, Wiltshire, Berkshire, and Kent. For large commercial and industrial projects, we operate UK-wide. If you're unsure, just ask.

Do you offer out-of-hours working?
Yes — evening and weekend working is available for commercial clients, retail premises, and any project where daytime access is not possible. Please mention this when you get in touch and we'll factor it into your programme.

I'm not sure which service I need — can you advise?
Absolutely. Just describe your surface and what you'd like to achieve — our team will recommend the correct coating system and explain all the options available to you.
