About Sparta Coatings
We are specialist on-site spray coating contractors with over 10 years of experience delivering professional, manufacturer-approved coating systems to residential, commercial, and industrial properties across the South of England and the wider UK.

We are not painters and decorators. We are specialist coating contractors — and there is a significant difference. Every surface we coat receives the correct, purpose-formulated product for that exact substrate, applied using professional airless or HVLP spray equipment by trained, experienced applicators who understand the science behind what they're doing as well as the craft.

Our Story
Sparta Coatings was founded on a single principle: that the UK's spray coating industry deserved a contractor that truly understood the products it was applying. Too many projects fail — coatings delaminate, peel, or fail prematurely — not because the products aren't good enough, but because the wrong product was used on the wrong substrate, or the preparation wasn't carried out correctly.

We set out to build a business where that never happens. Every project we take on is preceded by a proper condition survey. Every coating system is specified correctly for the substrate, environment, and required service life. Every stage of preparation is carried out to the appropriate standard. And every project is signed off with the manufacturer warranty the client was promised at the start.

That commitment has earned us approved applicator status with the UK's leading coating manufacturers — including HD Sharmans, Giromax, Liquisil, Kolorbond, Tikkurila, Jotun, Sherwin-Williams, Rust-Oleum Mathys, and others. It has also earned us the trust of facilities managers, property developers, house builders, commercial landlords, and homeowners who come back to us project after project.

What Makes Us Different
Approved Applicator Status
We hold approved applicator status with the UK's leading coating manufacturers across every category we work in. This means every project is not just guaranteed by us — it's warranted by the manufacturer.

Substrate-First Approach
Most spraying contractors use the same products across all surfaces. We don't. UPVC windows get Kolorbond. Kitchen cabinets get Tikkurila Helmi or Morrells. Cladding gets Temadur 20, Giromax, or Liquisil. Steel gets Jotun or Sherwin-Williams. The correct product for the correct surface — every time, without exception.

ISO 12944-Compliant Industrial Coatings
For commercial and industrial projects, every coating system is specified to the correct ISO 12944 corrosivity category for the building's environment — ensuring the protection level matches the exposure conditions your building actually faces.

Full Documentation on Every Project
Every Sparta Coatings project — however large or small — is supported by a method statement, COSHH assessment, surface preparation records, DFT (dry film thickness) readings where applicable, and a full sign-off document. Not because we have to, but because we believe our clients deserve to know exactly what was done to their building and to what standard.

Safety Without Compromise
All working-at-height operations are carried out in full compliance with the Work at Height Regulations 2005. We never work without appropriate access equipment, edge protection, and documented risk assessments — and we never cut corners on safety to save time.

Our Approved Applicator Status
Being an approved applicator is not simply about which products we buy — it requires training, assessment, and ongoing compliance with the manufacturer's specified application processes. Our approved applicator relationships include:

HD Sharmans — Temadur 20/50 wall cladding systems; Seamsil cut-edge corrosion systems

Giromax — BBA-certified polymer hybrid cladding and roof coating systems

Liquisil — 25-year guaranteed silicone metal coating and roof systems

Kolorbond — UPVC window, door, conservatory, fascia, and soffit coating systems

Tikkurila — Helmi kitchen and furniture lacquers; Otex Akva adhesion systems

Jotun — Industrial and structural steelwork coating systems

Sherwin-Williams — Industrial, commercial, and intumescent fire protection systems

Rust-Oleum / Mathys — Noxyde, CombiColor, Hard Hat, and Damp-Proof systems

Our Team
Sparta Coatings is built on the quality of its people. Every applicator who works on your project is trained, experienced, and genuinely cares about the quality of the finished result. We believe the preparation stage is where great finishes are made or lost — and our team treat it with the same care and attention as the spraying itself.

Our management team brings together expertise in:

Professional spray coating application and technique
